Crested Gecko

Crested Geckos do not have eyelids. They moisten their eyes by licking them.

  • Scientific Name: Correlophus Ciliatus
  • Average Adult Size: 5 to 8 inches long
  • Average Life Span: 15-30 years
  • Habitat: A warm, humid environment.

Crested Geckos were discovered in 1866 by Alphone Guichenot. They were thought to be extinct until they were "rediscovered" in New Caledonia in 1994. They are nocturnal. They eat fruits and bugs in the wild, and like to climb on trees.
